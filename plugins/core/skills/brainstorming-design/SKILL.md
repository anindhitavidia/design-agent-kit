# Brainstorming a design idea

Use this when a stakeholder has a rough product idea and you need to converge on a clear problem-and-approach before invoking `/design-kit:design-sprint` or `/design-kit:design-brief`.

## Process

1. Read `docs/context/personas.md` and `docs/context/brand.md` to anchor on who and how.
2. Ask clarifying questions one at a time. Multiple choice when possible. Focus on:
   - Who is this for, specifically?
   - What's the user trying to accomplish?
   - What constraints exist (time, scope, tech)?
   - What does success look like in 1 quarter?
3. Propose 2-3 approaches with trade-offs. Lead with your recommendation.
4. Once aligned, present a 1-paragraph summary the user can paste into `/design-kit:design-brief`.

Don't write code. Don't produce a full brief. The point is to converge on direction quickly.
